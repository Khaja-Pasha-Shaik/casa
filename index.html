House Rules

  • Cleanliness: Everyone is responsible for cleaning up after themselves in common areas.
  • Dishwashing: Dishwashing to be complete by EOD, dont postpone. Discipline is everything
  • Not Feeling well: Please transfer your cleaning duties to another member for that day.
  • Quiet Hours: From 10 PM to 7 AM, maintain a noise-free environment.
  • Shared Expenses: Please update the bills within 2 days of the expenditure. If the period is exceeded, provide a clear explanation to the relevant individuals as needed. If a proper justification is not given, or if the bill is unclear to others, the bill will be considered void, and the payee will be responsible for covering the cost.
  • No Improper Expenses: If you crave something or wish to buy something for personal use, please bear the cost yourself. Shared expenses should only cover mutually agreed-upon items.
  • Approvals for Splits: Any expense that requires a split among the members should receive necessary approvals beforehand. This isn't meant to limit anyone but ensures transparency and helps us manage our finances individually and responsibly.
  • Issue Resolution: If you have any issues, discuss them with the group on WhatsApp.

Payment Dates

  • Wi-Fi Payment: Due on the 10th of every month.
  • Electricity Payment: Due on the 10th of every month.
